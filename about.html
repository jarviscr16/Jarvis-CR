My Journey

From casual lockdown games to Champion — built step by step.

2020

Started playing Clash Royale during lockdown online classes — sharing one account with my brother and taking turns every match.

2021

Created my own account as interest grew and started understanding gameplay mechanics.

Burnout Phase

Got frustrated by pay-to-win gaps and temporarily stopped playing.

Return

Returned around 1.5 years ago with a new mindset — learning matchups, placements, and discipline.

2025

Reached 10K trophies in August 2025 and climbed to 15K shortly after.

Champion

Hit Champion last season — and now help players facing the same struggles I once had.
