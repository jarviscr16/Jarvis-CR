About the Coach

My journey wasn’t pay-to-win. It was learning, quitting, returning, and pushing through.

2020 — The Start

I started playing Clash Royale during COVID online classes. Me and my brother shared one account, playing one match each.

2021 — My Own Account

As my interest grew, I created my own account and began learning the game seriously.

The Burnout

I slowly understood the mechanics, but got frustrated by pay-to-win players and quit.

The Comeback

Around 1.5 years ago, I returned with a fresh mindset — focusing on fundamentals.

2025 — The Push

I hit 10K trophies in August 2025, quickly climbed to 15K, and reached Champion in the following season.

Why I Coach Now

I coach players between 0–10K trophies who are stuck where I once was — confused, tilted, and overwhelmed.

No shortcuts. Just understanding the game.
